Adaptivity
----------

In this lesson the nonlinear Poisson equation will be solved with adaptive mesh refinement,
introducing the relevant Dune and PDELab concepts for error estimation and mesh adaptation.
A written form of this tutorial is available :download:`for download <../pdfs/tutorial05.pdf>`.
You can also :download:`download the lecture slides <../pdfs/adaptivity_slides.pdf>`.
